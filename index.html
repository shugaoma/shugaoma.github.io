About

I am a research scientist at the Oculus Research Pittsburgh. Before this occupation, I graduated with Ph.D. degree from the Computer Science Department of Boston University, during which I was in the Image and Video Computing (IVC) group, advised by Prof. Stan Sclaroff . My research interest is in computer vision and machine learning, with a focus on human action recognition in videos and images. From 2006 to 2009, I worked on computer vision with Prof. Weiqiang Wang in the JDL in the Chinese Academy of Sciences, where I received my Master's degree. In 2006, I received Bachelor's Degree in Software Engineering from the Fudan University (China) and also Bachelor's Degree in Computer Sciences from the University College Dublin (Ireland).
