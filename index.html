About

I am a research scientist at the Facebook Reality Labs (FRL) Pittsburgh. Before joining FRL, I achieved my Ph.D degree in the Computer Science Department of Boston University, during which I was in the Image and Video Computing (IVC) group, advised by Prof. Stan Sclaroff . From 2006 to 2009, I worked on computer vision with Prof. Weiqiang Wang in the JDL in the Chinese Academy of Sciences, where I received my Master's degree. In 2006, I received Bachelor's Degree in Software Engineering from the Fudan University (China) and also Bachelor's Degree in Computer Sciences from the University College Dublin (Ireland).
